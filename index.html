    The City of Maryville Parks and Recreation Department strives to provide a variety of experiences and an enhanced quality of life, while adding to the city's economic base through tournaments, festivals, attractions, and programs. The park system is comprised of approximately 10 plus parks and related facilities. The recreation division coordinates a wide range of programs, instructional, developmental and leisure opportunities for all ages, interests and abilities. The department strives to provide the opportunity for each individual to have a healthy, active, safe, and enjoyable lifestyle.
